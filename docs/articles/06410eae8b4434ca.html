Mitigation

Additional mitigation guidance and recommended practices are publicly available on the ICS webpage at cisa.gov/ics in the technical information paper, ICS-TIP-12-146-01B--Targeted Cyber Intrusion Detection and Mitigation Strategies. The attacker's device must remain paired with and in proximity of the motorcycle for the entire duration of the firmware update. CISA encourages organizations to implement recommended cybersecurity strategies for proactive defense of ICS assets. The motorcycle must first be in Bluetooth pairing mode, and the attacker must be in proximity of the vehicle and understand the full pairing process, to be able to pair their device with the vehicle.
