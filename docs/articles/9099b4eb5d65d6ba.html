BOD 22-01 requires Federal Civilian Executive Branch (FCEB) agencies to remediate identified vulnerabilities by the due date to protect FCEB networks against active threats.

Apply vendor-supplied patches as soon as possible. If patching is not immediately feasible, implement recommended workarounds and compensating controls. Review endpoint detection rules and update threat intelligence feeds. Scan for known indicators of compromise.
